Chicken Ramen recipe

This creamy chicken ramen elevates humble ramen to new heights with a simple but tasty broth.

Ingredients

Ingredients for the chicken:

  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 skinless, boneless chicken breast
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
Ingredients for the broth:
  • 1 1/2 cloves garlic, minced or equivalent garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili flakes
  • 2 tablespoons marinara sauce
  • 3/4 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 1/4 cups chicken stock, divided
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 packages ramen noodles, seasoning packet reserved for another use
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 green onion, sliced
  • cilantro leaves
