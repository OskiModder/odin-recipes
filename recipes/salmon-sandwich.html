Salmon Sandwich recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 slices whole wheat bread
  • 1 tablespoon cream cheese
  • 1 tablespoon creamy goat cheese (Optional)

  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon dried dill, or to taste
